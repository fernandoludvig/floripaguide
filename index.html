Florianópolis

Known as Ilha da Magia and affectionately called Floripa, it has about 500 thousand inhabitants and is around 54 km from North to South. Is a captivating island city located in southern Brazil. Every little piece of Florianópolis has a distinct characteristic, and its main attraction is always Nature, in its most diverse forms, especially through the sea and its mountains. With its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes, it attracts tourists from all over the world. If you're planning to visit this enchanting destination, look no further than Floripa Guide. This comprehensive website is your ultimate resource for exploring Florianopolis, offering a wealth of information on everything, from the best beaches to hidden gems.

What to do in Florianópolis?

Florianopolis is a city known for its beautiful beaches and outdoor adventures. The beaches in the city are highly recommended, and if you want more information about them, click here. Apart from the beaches, there are various activities to enjoy, such as take a stroll along Beira-Mar Norte, where you can admire a beautiful sunset and the Hercílio Luz Bridge, a postcard-worthy landmark of the capital, sandboarding, boating, outdoor sports, and exploring Azorean villages, which have a rich cultural heritage. Florianopolis has a wide range of outdoor activities to offer, making it an ideal destination for adventure enthusiasts.
